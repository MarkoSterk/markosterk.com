About me

I am a PhD candidate and researcher at the University of Maribor in Maribor, Slovenia. My research is focused on the modeling of cellular processes, time series and statistical analysis, complex systems, and networks, with an emphasis on calcium wave propagation in cellular networks. Part of my research effort is also focused on the development of new and efficient algorithms for calcium wave detection and characterisation with special attention given to the roles of individual cells in the network.

Another passion is related to the web development. I am primarily focused on back-end web development in Python (Flask) and Node.JS (express), however, I have experience with other frameworks, e.g. Django and FastAPI. My background in physics has given me the necessary analytical skills for development and troubleshooting of web applications.
